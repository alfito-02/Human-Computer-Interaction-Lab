About Us

Cateringz started as a humble homemade culinary venture and has grown into a favorite choice for exceptional food at various events across Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang. Our journey began in a small kitchen where we honed our craft, and today we are celebrated for curating delightful culinary experiences for all types of gatherings. We prioritize using the finest ingredients, sourcing locally to ensure freshness and sustainability. By using seasonal items, we not only support local farmers and artisans but also enhance the flavors in each dish. Our dedicated chefs work closely with these local producers to deliver the best taste and quality in every bite.
